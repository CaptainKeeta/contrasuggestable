What is Li-Fi?


How does Li-Fi work?


'Li-Fi is the use of the visible light specturm instead of radio frequencies to enable wireless communication.'

-pureLiFi company home page



Li-Fi can be applied to any LED light bulb, using a lamp 'driver' to flash the light at speeds incapable of being seen by the naked eye.

Li-Fi is the next generation of wireless data transfer.



As it uses light waves rather than radio waves, wireless data transfer is much faster and efficient


Need for Speed

Regular Wi-Fi will hit limits at about 150 Mps, but is expensive and inefficent to acheive. Li-Fi's theoretical maximum is 224 Gps, over 1400% faster!

With this speed, a person with capable hardware could download 100 1.5 Gigabyte movies in one second.
